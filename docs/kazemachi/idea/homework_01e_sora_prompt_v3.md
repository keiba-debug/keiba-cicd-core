## A. メインプロンプト（ベース画像）

```
manga-influenced anime, sketchy pen linework, flat color, low saturation, muted tones, white background, upper body shot

1girl, solo, age 26, slim build, slightly slouched posture — not cool, not confident, just a person standing there with too many thoughts

HAIR: black, messy chin-length bob — NOT styled, NOT fashionable. hair that was cut short for convenience and hasn't been brushed properly today. some strands sticking up, some falling across her forehead unevenly. no hair clip (the color accent comes from the racing form, not from accessories)

FACE — THIS IS THE HARDEST PART:
- this is NOT a cool girl. this is NOT a confident girl. this is a girl who SOMETIMES gets it right and doesn't fully understand why
- mouth: closed, lips pressed together slightly — she is holding something back. not a smile. not a frown. the mouth of someone who wants to say "I think it's this horse" but is afraid of being wrong again
- eyes: looking slightly downward or to the side — NOT making eye contact with anyone. her eyes are soft, slightly unfocused, as if she's listening to something inside her own head. there is visible tension in her eyebrows — not angry, just... unsure
- cheek blush: 2-3 short sketch lines on one cheek — the blush of someone who gets embarrassed easily, not the blush of cuteness
- overall expression: imagine someone at a racetrack who just circled a horse on their form and immediately thought "wait, was that right?" — that EXACT moment of second-guessing your own gut feeling

CLOTHING:
- a slightly oversized button-up shirt in dusty blue-gray (NOT bright blue, NOT navy — a color that looks like it was washed too many times). the shirt is wrinkled, untucked on one side, sleeves rolled up unevenly — one sleeve higher than the other
- plain white t-shirt visible underneath at the collar
- faded jeans with a small dirt mark near one knee (she knelt at the paddock fence)
- the overall clothing impression: "she got dressed in a hurry and didn't check a mirror"

CRITICAL PROP — BINOCULARS:
- compact old binoculars hanging from a worn fabric strap around her neck
- the binoculars look used — not new, not shiny. scratched lens caps, faded strap
- these binoculars are HER tool. she uses them at the paddock every week. they are an extension of her eyes

POSE:
- standing, weight on one leg, the other foot turned slightly inward (a subtle pigeon-toe — not cute, just a natural habit of someone who's lost in thought)
- RIGHT hand holds a folded racing form — the paper is covered in COLORFUL pen marks: red circles, blue arrows, green underlines. the marks are messy, impulsive, overlapping. some things are circled AND crossed out
- LEFT hand is touching her own neck or collarbone area — a self-soothing gesture. she does this when she's nervous about her pick
- she is NOT looking at the racing form. she has already made her marks. she is looking away — at nothing in particular — processing

THE RACING FORM:
- folded newspaper-style sheet with visible grid/table structure
- the colored pen marks (red, blue, green) are the MOST VIVID elements in the entire image
- the marks should look like they were made fast, without thinking — because they were. Sora marks instinctively, then doubts afterward
- important: the marks look almost childish in their messiness. this is not analysis. this is feeling made visible

COLOR PALETTE — CRITICAL FOR CAST UNITY:
- the shirt is dusty blue-gray — similar value range to MC's light blue shirt but slightly different hue (MC = clean light blue, Sora = washed-out dusty blue)
- jeans are faded, desaturated
- skin tones are muted
- the ONLY saturated colors are the pen marks on the racing form
- when placed next to MC (light blue shirt, clipboard, half-lidded eyes), they should look like they belong in the same room, drawn by the same artist, in the same color world

ART STYLE — MATCH MC EXACTLY:
- linework: sketchy, imperfect pen strokes. visible line weight variation. lines that don't quite connect. the kind of lines you draw when you're sketching fast in a notebook
- proportions: manga-influenced deformation — head slightly oversized, features simplified. NOT realistic. NOT polished
- coloring: flat fill, almost no shading or gradient. watercolor-wash feeling in some areas is OK
- facial features: MINIMAL. small eyes (NOT large anime eyes). simple nose (dot or short line). emotion comes from eyebrow angle and mouth shape, nothing else
- background: plain white or very light warm gray
- this should look like a CHARACTER SHEET from a manga artist's sketchbook, not a finished illustration

DO NOT:
- make her look cool or composed
- give her a confident smirk or knowing smile
- make her eyes sharp or determined
- make the shirt look neat or intentionally styled
- use bright or saturated colors anywhere except the racing form pen marks
- give her a fashionable hairstyle
- make her look like she knows what she's doing. she DOESN'T. she just FEELS things, and sometimes she's right
```

## 設計メモ

### v2 → v3 の変更点

| 項目 | v2 | v3 | 理由 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 服装 | トラックジャケット（紺白） | くすんだ青灰シャツ（着崩し） | ジャケットが派手すぎた。シャツなら彩度を落としやすい |
| 髪色 | ダークブラウン（ほぼ黒） | 黒で確定 | MCとの区別はシルエット（ボブの長さ・乱れ具合）で出す |
| ヘアクリップ | ホログラフィック | なし | 彩度ポイントは出馬表の色ペンに集約。装飾品を減らして「構わない子」感を出す |
| 表情 | 静かな自信（90%自信） | 自信はあるが危うい（60%自信40%不安） | 「かっこいい系」から「危うい系」へ |
| ポーズ | 片手ポケット（余裕） | 首元に手を当てる（不安の仕草） | ボディランゲージで脆さを出す |
| DO NOT | 少年漫画・キラキラ目の禁止 | 「かっこいい」「余裕」「知的」の禁止を追加 | AIが「かっこいい系」に引っ張られるのを防ぐ |

### ソラの「色」は何か

v2では「ヘアクリップが唯一の彩度」だった。v3では**「出馬表の色ペンが唯一の彩度」に変更**。

理由：ソラの「色」は外見ではなく仕事に出る。おしゃれに興味がない子が、出馬表だけは3色のペンで塗りたくる。**その落差がソラの人格**。外見は地味、仕事は派手。

### MCとの視覚比較（v3版）

| | MC | ソラ |
|---|---|---|
| 髪 | 黒・肩まで・まっすぐ | 黒・顎まで・無造作 |
| 服の色 | 水色シャツ（きれい） | くすんだ青灰シャツ（よれよれ） |
| 小道具 | クリップボード（整理された紙） | 出馬表（ぐちゃぐちゃな色ペン） |
| 表情 | 半目（「またか」） | 不安混じり（「合ってるかな」） |
| 体温 | 低い（観察者） | やや高い（当事者） |

同じ「青系シャツの黒髪の女」だが、**MCは「整っている」、ソラは「崩れている」**。この対比で区別がつく。

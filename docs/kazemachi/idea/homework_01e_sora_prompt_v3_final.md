# ソラ（直感派）— 最終プロンプト v3 final

> v3の通常立ち版は「危うさ」がまだ足りなかった。
> レイの指摘：ソラの脆さは「自分の直感を信じたいのに、信じる根拠がない」こと。
> それを表情とボディランゲージの両方に落とし込む最終調整。

---

## A. 通常ベース画像（脆さ強化版）

```
manga-influenced anime, sketchy pen linework, flat color, low saturation, muted tones, white background, upper body shot

1girl, solo, age 26, slim build

HAIR: black, messy chin-length bob — unwashed, unkempt, some strands stuck to her cheek, some sticking out at odd angles. this is hair that has been nervously tugged at

CLOTHING:
- wrinkled dusty blue-gray button-up shirt, open, untucked, sleeves rolled up unevenly
- plain white t-shirt underneath
- old scratched compact binoculars hanging from worn strap around neck
- faded dirty jeans

POSE — VULNERABILITY IS IN THE BODY:
- standing, but NOT upright — shoulders slightly hunched forward, like she's trying to make herself smaller
- right hand holds a folded racing form covered in messy colorful pen marks (red circles, blue X marks, green underlines — chaotic, overlapping)
- left hand is gripping her own upper arm — a self-hugging gesture. she is holding herself. this is what people do when they feel exposed
- her weight is on one leg, the other knee is slightly bent inward
- overall posture: someone standing at the edge of a group, not in the center

EXPRESSION — THE CORE OF THIS CHARACTER:
- she is looking down and slightly to the side — NOT at the racing form, NOT at the viewer. at nothing. at the floor. at her own thoughts
- her mouth is closed but her lips are pressed together tightly — holding something in. words she wants to say but won't
- her eyebrows are slightly raised in the center and lowered at the edges — the OPPOSITE of anger. this is worry. this is "did I get it wrong again?"
- eyes: not wide, not narrow — slightly downcast. there is something tired in them. not sleepy-tired. tired of doubting herself
- minimal blush — just 2 short sketch lines on one cheek
- the overall expression is: someone who made a decision 10 minutes ago and has been quietly panicking ever since

WHAT THIS IMAGE SHOULD MAKE THE VIEWER FEEL:
- "I want to tell her it's going to be okay"
- "she looks like she might be right but she doesn't know it yet"
- NOT "she's cool" NOT "she's cute" NOT "she's confident"
- the feeling is SYMPATHY mixed with CURIOSITY

THE RACING FORM:
- the colored pen marks (red, blue, green) are the most vivid elements in the image
- the marks are messy and impulsive — some things circled AND crossed out
- the paper is slightly crumpled from being gripped too tightly

ART STYLE — MATCH MC:
- sketchy imperfect pen linework, visible line weight variation
- flat coloring, almost no shading, watercolor-wash texture OK
- very low saturation everywhere EXCEPT racing form pen marks
- manga-influenced proportions — head slightly oversized, simplified features
- minimal facial detail — emotion conveyed through few lines
- plain white background
- looks like a page from a slice-of-life manga

DO NOT:
- make her stand up straight with good posture
- give her a smirk, smile, or any expression of confidence
- make her eyes sharp or determined
- make her look at the viewer
- make her look cool, composed, or fashionable
- use bright colors except on the racing form
```

## B. 立ち版・迷い（レース前の普段使い）

```
（Aに以下を差し替え）

POSE:
- standing, leaning against a wall or railing (just her back touching it, casual lean)
- right hand holds the racing form at her side, dangling loosely — she's not looking at it
- left hand is near her mouth — thumb touching her lower lip, a thinking habit. she bites her thumbnail when she's nervous (don't show biting, just the hand near the mouth)
- she is looking upward at an angle — at the sky, at the ceiling, at the memory of a horse she saw at the paddock 20 minutes ago
- her eyes are slightly unfocused — she is replaying something in her head

EXPRESSION:
- mouth slightly open behind her hand — she just exhaled
- eyes looking up but not bright — the look of someone trying to remember a feeling, not a fact
- "was it the way that horse moved, or am I imagining it?"
- the viewer should not be able to tell if she's confident or terrified. BOTH should be possible from this face

（この版のポイント：ソラの直感は「思い出す」作業。
データを見返すナルミと違い、ソラは自分の感覚を信じるために
「あの時の感じ」を何度も頭の中で再生する。
その再生中の顔は、自信にも不安にも見える。）
```

## レイの仕掛け追加メモ（生成後に検討）

この画像群で「脆さ」が確立されたあと、レイが仕込むべき層：

**ソラが「自分を抱えている」ポーズのとき、ジャケットの袖がめくれて手首が見える。**
手首には何もない。——が、「何かを隠しているのでは？」と考察班が推測する余地を残す。
実際は何もない。ソラはただ癖で腕を触る子。
でも「何もないこと」を証明するのは難しい。考察班は探し続ける。

**これは仕掛けではなく「仕掛けに見える日常」。** レイの最も高度な設計：何もないところに意味を見出そうとする読者の行為自体がコンテンツになる。

# ゲンさん（経験派）— v2 アプローチ逆転版

> v1の失敗：「穏やかな人に鋭さを足す」→「ちょっと眠そうなおじいちゃん」
> v2の方針：「鋭い男を描いて、表面に穏やかさを被せる」
> ソラの教訓：感情の核を逆から攻める。

---

## ゲンさんの再定義

v1：「穏やかなおじいちゃんだけど目だけ鋭い」
→ AIの解釈：穏やかなおじいちゃん（目が少し細い）

**v2：「40年分の修羅場を見てきた男が、今は穏やかに振る舞っている」**
→ ベースは修羅場を知っている男。表面だけ柔らかい。

参考イメージ：
- 引退した刑事が近所のおじさんをやっている感じ
- 笑っているが、目だけは「お前のこと全部見えてるぞ」と言っている
- 町内会では人気者。でも一対一になると少し怖い

## A. メインプロンプト（鋭さベース版）

```
manga-influenced anime, sketchy pen linework, flat color, low saturation, muted tones, white background, upper body shot

1boy, solo, early 60s, solid build — broad shoulders, thick arms, barrel chest. this is NOT a frail old man. this is a man who could still grab you by the collar if he wanted to. he just chooses not to

HAIR: short gray hair, thinning slightly at the crown but not bald. unkempt — he runs his hand through it when he's thinking, so it sticks up in odd places. white stubble on jaw and chin — he shaves every other day at best

FACE — START FROM SHARPNESS, ADD WARMTH ON TOP:

Step 1 — THE BASE IS A SHARP MAN:
- this man has SEEN things. 40 years of racetracks, bars, wins, losses, arguments, debts, friendships, betrayals. his face carries ALL of it
- his eyes are the eyes of someone who reads people for a living. they are OPEN — not squinting, not narrow. OPEN and WATCHFUL. the irises are small and dark. he is looking at you and he already knows what you're going to say
- his jaw is set. his brow is slightly heavy. there are deep vertical lines between his eyebrows from decades of concentrating

Step 2 — THE MASK IS WARMTH:
- OVER this sharp base, he has learned to smile. the smile is REAL but it is CHOSEN. he smiles because he decided to be kind, not because he is naturally soft
- the corners of his mouth turn up. the laugh lines around his mouth are deep and genuine
- but the smile does NOT reach his eyes. his eyes remain watchful, assessing, calm
- the overall effect: a man who looks friendly until you look at his eyes for too long

CLOTHING — COOLER PALETTE:
- a plain dark gray-green work shirt — NOT flannel, NOT plaid. a simple, sturdy shirt that a stable hand or a track worker might wear. rolled sleeves showing thick forearms
- or: a faded olive-drab utility vest over a plain dark t-shirt — the vest has pen marks on the chest pocket where he clips his pen
- white undershirt visible at the collar
- the colors should be COOL and MUTED — gray-green, olive, dark charcoal. NOT warm browns, NOT cozy flannels
- the clothing says "working man" not "cozy grandpa"

HANDS:
- thick fingers, scarred knuckles, visible veins on forearms
- one hand holds a pen — not delicately, but gripped like a tool
- hands that have gripped railings, grabbed shoulders, pounded tables

PROP — THE NOTEBOOK (simplified):
- a worn spiral notebook, open, on the table or in his lap
- Japanese handwriting visible — fast, messy
- a red circle on one entry — the ONLY vivid color in the image
- the notebook is functional, not decorative — stained cover, bent pages

SECONDARY PROPS:
- reading glasses pushed up on forehead
- smartphone face-down on table (he doesn't use it)

POSE:
- sitting, leaning forward with forearms on the table — the lean of someone who is INTERESTED, not relaxed
- his head is slightly tilted — evaluating
- one hand near the notebook, the other resting on the table — he might pick up the pen at any moment
- he is looking at something (or someone) across the table — NOT at the camera, NOT at the notebook
- his body says "I'm listening" but his eyes say "I already know"

WHAT THIS IMAGE SHOULD MAKE THE VIEWER FEEL:
- "I wouldn't want to play poker against this guy"
- "he's smiling, but why do I feel like he's testing me?"
- "friendly, but there's something underneath"
- like meeting a retired detective at a neighborhood bar — you feel safe, but also seen

ART STYLE — CRITICAL FOR CAST UNITY:
- MUST match MC and Sora in linework: sketchy, imperfect pen strokes, rough edges
- flat coloring with watercolor-wash texture
- very low saturation — the only vivid color is the red circle in the notebook
- skin tone: weathered but NOT orange/warm. use a more neutral, desaturated skin tone
- the overall color temperature should lean COOL, not warm
- background: plain white
- this should look like it belongs on the same page as MC (blue shirt, half-lidded eyes) and Sora (gray shirt, binoculars, racing form)

DO NOT:
- make him look gentle, kind, grandfatherly, or cozy
- start from warmth and add sharpness — START FROM SHARPNESS
- use warm brown/orange color palette
- use plaid flannel or any "cozy cabin" clothing
- make his eyes squinted shut or nearly closed
- make him look sleepy, drowsy, or content
- give him a warm, inviting smile — his smile should have an edge
- make the linework smooth or clean — keep it rough and sketchy
- make him look like he belongs in a different art style than MC and Sora
```

## C. バリエーション：本性全開（レース中・レアカード）

```
（Aのポーズ・表情を以下に差し替え）

POSE:
- standing, or half-standing from his chair — one hand gripping the table edge
- the notebook is forgotten on the table
- his reading glasses have fallen down to his nose or are hanging off one ear
- he is looking straight ahead with FULL INTENSITY

EXPRESSION:
- the warmth-mask is COMPLETELY OFF
- mouth open — not shouting yet, but about to. jaw is tight
- eyes WIDE OPEN — the calculating look has become CERTAINTY
- this is the face of a man who KNOWS what is about to happen because he has SEEN this exact pattern before
- 40 years of experience are compressing into this single second
- he looks like a different person from the base image — and that is the point

（この版は「ゲンさんの本性」。初めて見た人は「え、あの穏やかな人が？」となる。
ベース画像との差が大きいほどギャップが効く。
使用頻度：低い。G1レースの佳境でだけ使う。）
```

## 設計メモ

### v1 → v2 の根本的な変更

| | v1 | v2 |
|---|---|---|
| 出発点 | 穏やかさ | 鋭さ |
| 表面 | 穏やかさ（そのまま） | 穏やかさ（後から被せる） |
| 結果 | 穏やかなおじいちゃん | 鋭い男が穏やかに振る舞っている |
| AIへの指示 | 「優しいけど目は鋭く」 | 「鋭い男を描け。口だけ笑わせろ」 |

### 色温度の修正

| キャラ | 服の色 | 全体の温度 |
|---|---|---|
| MC | 水色シャツ | 寒色 |
| ソラ | くすんだ青灰シャツ | 中間（やや寒） |
| ナルミ | 紺カーディガン | 寒色 |
| テツ | モノクロのコート | 無彩色 |
| ゲンさん v1 | 茶色フランネル | **暖色 ← 浮く** |
| **ゲンさん v2** | **暗いグレーグリーン/オリーブ** | **中間（やや寒）← 馴染む** |

### 「引退した刑事」のイメージで統一

ゲンさんのビジュアルコンセプトを「近所のおじいちゃん」から**「引退した刑事が競馬場に通っている」**に変更。

- 現場を知っている男の体格（ゴツい、デスクワーク体型ではない）
- 人を読む目（開いた目、小さな瞳孔）
- でも今は穏やか（笑顔は本物。ただし目は止まらない）
- 服装は機能的で地味（おしゃれではない。清潔でもない。ただ着ている）

## A. ベース画像（静かな笑みの仮面版）

```
manga-influenced anime, sketchy pen linework, flat color, low saturation, muted tones, white background, upper body shot, subtle shadows on face

1boy, solo, early 60s, solid stocky build — broad shoulders, thick neck, barrel chest. a man built by 40 years of standing at racetracks in sun and rain

HAIR: short gray-white hair, slightly messy and thinning. white stubble on jaw and chin — 2-3 days unshaved. not stylish, not groomed — functional

FACE — TWO LAYERS (THIS IS THE CORE):

Layer 1 — THE MOUTH (warmth, surface):
- mouth CLOSED. lips together. ONLY the right corner of the mouth is slightly raised — a HALF-smile, asymmetric
- this is NOT a full smile. it is the smile of a man who is amused by something he hasn't shared yet
- deep laugh lines around the mouth — these are real, earned from decades of drinking and laughing. but RIGHT NOW he is not laughing

Layer 2 — THE EYES (sharpness, underneath):
- eyes are OPEN at normal width but the IRISES are small and dark — focused, calculating
- the eyes are NOT smiling. the eyes are WATCHING. comparing. measuring
- there are SHADOWS under the eyes — slight dark circles, fatigue lines. not from lack of sleep, but from decades of concentrating
- the lower eyelids are very slightly raised — the micro-expression of someone EVALUATING what they see
- the gaze is directed slightly past the viewer or at the notebook — NOT a warm direct look
- subtle shadow along the nose bridge and beside the mouth — gives WEIGHT to the face even though he looks friendly

THE DISCONNECT BETWEEN MOUTH AND EYES IS EVERYTHING:
- if both are warm → failed (nice grandpa)
- if both are sharp → failed (manga villain)
- mouth warm + eyes cold/calculating = CORRECT (retired detective)

SKIN: sun-weathered, rough texture. NOT smooth. visible pores and unevenness. the face of a man who has spent thousands of hours outdoors. skin tone is neutral/desaturated — NOT orange, NOT pink

CLOTHING:
- dark olive-green plaid work shirt — the plaid pattern is subtle, muted. the shirt is WORN: fabric is soft from hundreds of washes, slightly faded at the shoulders and elbows
- sleeves rolled up unevenly to just below elbows
- plain white undershirt visible at the open collar
- the shirt has a chest pocket with a pen clipped to it — ink stain visible near the pocket edge

HANDS — TELL THE STORY OF 40 YEARS:
- thick fingers, wide palms — hands that have gripped railings in every weather
- knuckles are prominent, slightly swollen — old man's hands that have seen work
- visible veins on the back of the hands and forearms
- skin on hands is rough, dry — small nicks and calluses
- one hand holds a pen loosely between index and middle finger — the pen is LIFTED off the page (he stopped writing)
- the other hand rests flat on the table or near the notebook — fingers slightly spread
- forearms are thick, tanned, with visible veins and sparse gray hair

PROP — THE NOTEBOOK:
- a well-worn spiral notebook, open on the table
- the page shows: Japanese handwriting (messy, fast, written for himself), a hand-drawn racecourse oval diagram, scattered numbers (times, horse numbers), margin notes
- ONE red circle around a small text entry — the ONLY vivid red in the entire image
- the notebook cover is bent, pages dog-eared and slightly yellowed
- the notebook should be CLEARLY VISIBLE — it is the second focal point after the face

SECONDARY PROPS:
- reading glasses pushed up on forehead (he forgets they're there)
- smartphone lying FACE-DOWN on the table (he gave up on it years ago)

POSE:
- sitting at a simple table, upper body leaning slightly forward
- his posture suggests he was writing in the notebook but STOPPED — something caught his attention
- his head is tilted very slightly — the angle of recognition, of déjà vu
- he is looking at something (the notebook, or something across the table) — NOT directly at the camera
- overall body language: "I just noticed something, but I'm not going to say it yet"

LIGHTING AND SHADOW — CRITICAL FOR "WEIGHT":
- the overall image is low-contrast, but there ARE subtle shadows:
  - under the eyes (fatigue/depth)
  - along the nose bridge (structure)
  - under the chin (weight)
  - beside the mouth lines (age)
- these shadows are NOT dramatic. they are soft, sketchy — done with slightly darker pencil lines or light watercolor wash
- the shadows make a friendly face feel HEAVY. this is a man with history written on his face

ART STYLE — MATCH MC AND SORA:
- sketchy imperfect pen linework — same as MC (book-pile half-smile) and Sora (racing-form "...eh?")
- flat coloring with watercolor-wash texture in shadows
- very low saturation — only vivid color is the red circle in notebook
- manga-influenced proportions — slightly stylized, not realistic
- white background, no environment
- must look like the SAME ARTIST drew MC, Sora, and Gen-san

DO NOT:
- make him smile with teeth showing or mouth open
- make both eyes and mouth warm/friendly at the same time
- make his eyes squinted shut or narrow to slits
- make his eyes wide open in surprise
- make his hands thin, smooth, or delicate
- give him earrings or jewelry of any kind
- use warm brown/orange overall color palette
- use clean smooth digital linework
- make the expression intense, aggressive, or villainous
- make the notebook text in English
- make the face uniformly bright with no shadows

Negative prompt: earring, jewelry, open mouth, teeth showing, laughing, shouting, bright warm lighting, orange skin, smooth skin, clean hands, young hands, digital linework, english text
```

## B. バリエーション：「ペンが止まった瞬間」（0.5秒前）

```
（Aのポーズ・表情を以下に差し替え）

POSE:
- sitting at the table, was writing in the notebook — but his pen hand has STOPPED
- the pen tip is lifted 1cm off the page — frozen mid-stroke
- his other hand is still on the table, fingers curled slightly
- his HEAD has not moved yet — he is still looking down at the notebook
- but his EYES have shifted — they are no longer reading the page. they are looking at nothing. or at a memory
- his body is still in "writing mode" but his mind has LEFT

EXPRESSION:
- the half-smile is GONE. mouth is neutral, lips pressed together
- eyes are slightly unfocused — the gaze has gone INWARD
- this is the face of a man who just heard a chord of music he recognizes but can't name yet
- "この展開…どこかで…" — the thought is forming but has no words yet
- the viewer should feel: "something just happened inside this man's head"

（この版がカイの言う「0.5秒前」。ペンが止まって、目が泳いで、まだ口は開かない。
次の瞬間、ゲンさんは顔を上げて「ちょっと待って」と言う。でもこの画像はその直前。）
```

## C. バリエーション：本性（レース中レアカード）

```
（Aのポーズ・表情を以下に差し替え）

POSE:
- sitting but upper body lunged forward — both hands gripping the table edge
- the notebook is pushed aside, still open
- reading glasses have fallen to the tip of his nose or are hanging off one ear
- he is looking straight ahead — at a screen, at the track, at the finish line

EXPRESSION:
- the warm mask is COMPLETELY OFF
- mouth slightly open — jaw tight, about to shout but hasn't yet
- eyes WIDE and LOCKED — the calculating look has become CERTAINTY
- the shadows on his face are deeper in this version — the weight of 40 years is visible
- this is NOT anger. this is RECOGNITION. he has seen this exact pattern before and it is happening again RIGHT NOW
- the viewer should feel: "this is a completely different person from image A"

（使用頻度：低。G1の直線でだけ使う。
ベース版とのギャップが大きいほどインパクトがある。）
```
